    Educational Consultancy

    Committed to nurturing young tech-savvy talents, Elite Intellisense Ltd has special arrangement for institutions of learning and partners with schools to establish Digital Clubs at all academic institutional levels. These clubs equip students with various IT skills, instilling an early passion for technology and programming. These are some of our clubs:

      Coding Clubs:

      Join our Coding Club and unlock the world of programming! Learn how to create websites, mobile apps, and software. Develop problem-solving skills and enhance your logical thinking through fun coding challenges and interactive projects

      Animation Clubs:

      Discover the magic of animation in our Animation Club! Unleash your creativity by learning the art of storytelling through captivating visual effects and motion graphics. Explore various animation techniques and create your own animated stories and characters.

      Graphic Design Club:

      Dive into the world of visual communication with our Graphic Design Club! Develop your design skills and learn how to create eye-catching posters, logos, and digital artwork. Master the use of industry-standard design software and unleash your artistic potential.

      Game Development Club:

      Level up your gaming skills in our Game Development Club! Explore the fundamentals of game design and development, from concept creation to coding and digital artwork. Create your own interactive games and learn about the exciting world of game mechanics and storytelling.

      Robotics Club:

      Step into the future with our Robotics Club! Explore the principles of robotics and automation through hands-on projects and challenges. Learn to build and program robots, understand artificial intelligence, and develop problem-solving skills crucial for the future of technology.

      Photograph and video production Club:

      Capture moments and unleash your storytelling skills in our Photography/Video Production Club! Learn the art of photography, videography, and video editing. Discover how to create compelling visual narratives and develop skills that can pave the way for a successful career in media and entertainment.
